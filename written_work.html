OUR PROPOSAL OBJECTIVE

To develop an innovative social media platform that combines the experience of listening to music & social communication, allowing users to:

  • Listen to music in a own way.
  • Chat with friends in real-time while they enjoy musical content.
  • Send sound stickers that combine visual & auditory elements.
  • Create a community centered on musical exchange & creative communication.

Mission

Is revolutionize the way of people connect through music, creating a net-space where musical entertainment & social communication are same.

Vision

Be the leading platform that transforms the social musical experience, where every conversation is an opportunity to discover new music & every song is a way to connect with others.

COMMUNITY RULES

  1. Respect & Tolerance: Be nice with the community. Discriminatory comments based on race, gender, religion, or musical preferences are not tolerated. Respect different cultures & musical genres.
  2. Copyright: Respect the copyright of artists & composers. Only share music with appropriate licenses. Report any content that infringes copyright.
  3. Privacy & Security: Report any harassing or inappropriate behavior. Use the blocking function when necessary.
  4. Responsible Use: Respect volume limits in group chats. Contribute positively to conversations & musical discoveries.
  5. Moderation: Moderators have the authority to enforce these rules. Violations may result in warnings, suspensions, or permanent expulsion from the app. Users are encouraged to report infractions.

WHAT MAKES IT UNIQUE?

The app mixes music with social interaction in new ways. Friends can listen to songs together at the same time, chat while the music plays, & even build playlists as a group. It also has sound stickers that combine images with sounds, users can design their own while getting new ones updated often. Also, there are badges & rankings for users who introduce popular music to the community. The technology helps too, with AI that learns from social interactions to suggest music & playlist, high-quality audio, & smooth group listening without lag. The platform also connects people around the world, offering concerts, cultural music exchanges, & ways to discover new artists. Finally, it takes care of safety with smart filters, parental controls, & volume alerts to protect users.

DISTINCTIVE TECHNICAL FEATURES

  • Intuitive Interface: Music-centered design with easy-access controls. Smooth navigation between chat & music playback. Visual customization based on preferred musical genres.
  • Multi-platform Connectivity: Synchronization between mobile devices, web, & smart speakers. Integration with existing music services.
  • Continuous Innovation: Regular updates based on community feedback. Beta testing with active users. Collaboration with music & social technology experts.
